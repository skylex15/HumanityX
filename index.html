What is HumanityX?

HumanityX ($HMX) is a deflationary token with a built-in charity mechanism. Every transaction allocates funds to charitable causes while rewarding token holders and increasing scarcity. Our vision is to combine the power of blockchain with humanitarian efforts to make a tangible impact on global challenges.

Charity In Action

Your contributions drive real change. HumanityX funds a variety of charitable initiatives including:

Food Drives

Organizing food distribution for communities in need.

Shelters

Providing safe havens and support for the homeless.

Missionary Work

Supporting global outreach and humanitarian missions.

Community Governance

At HumanityX, token holders have a voice. Every quarter, $HMX holders vote on which causes and projects should receive funding. Your vote helps determine the allocation of charity funds and directs the future of humanitarian aid.
